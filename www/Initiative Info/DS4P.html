Data Segmentation for Privacy (DS4P)

Initiative Description: The Data Segmentation for Privacy initiative enables the implementation and management of disclosure policies that originate from the patient, the law, or an organization, in an interoperable manner within an electronic health information exchange environment, so that individually identifiable health information may be appropriately shared for:

  1. Patient treatment and care coordination
  2. Third party payment
  3. Analysis and reporting for operations, utilization, access quality and outcomes
  4. Public health reporting
  5. Population health, technology assessment and research

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Patient consent for sharing certain sensitive health information (MU04)
  • Protect electronic health information created or maintained by the CEHRT through the implementation of appropriate technical capabilities.
Standards in use
  • IHE XD* Profiles
    • IHE XDR and XDM Metadata
  • Vocabularies
    • ASC X12 5010
    • Healthcare Facility Type Value Set as defined in HITSP C80
    • HL7 RefrainPolicy
    • HL7 PurposeofUse
    • HL7 BasicConfidentialityCodeKind
    • HL7 ObligationCode
    • HL7 ActPolicyType
    • HL7 SensitivityPrivacyPolicy
  • Transport
    • SOAP
    • SMTP and S/MIME
  • Conveying Identity
    • Cross-Enterprise User Assertion (XUA)
    • OASIS SAML Specification Version 2.0
    • X.509 Digital Certificates
  • Patient Consent Structure
    • HL7 CDA R2 Consent Directive (DSTU)
