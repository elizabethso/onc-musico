Longitudinal Coordination of Care (LCC)

Initiative Description: LCC is a community-led initiative with multiple public and private sector partners, each committed to overcoming interoperability challenges in long-term, post-acute care (LTPAC) transitions. The LCC WG has developed a care plan glossary, the LCC Use Case 1.0, high level implementation guide, and several artifacts to help reduce the barriers of exchanging care plans between providers and between providers and patients. The initiative is currently working on a use case on interoperable care plan exchange which will feed into an implementation guide to be balloted through HL7 and then piloted by the community.

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Care Coordination
    • Summary of Care (SGRP303)
    • Referral Reports (SGRP305)
    • Interdisciplinary Problem List (SGRP127; future state)
    • Care Plan (SGRP304; future)
  • Improve Quality
    • New demographics (SGRP104)
    • Advanced Directive (SGRP112)
    • Family History (SGRP119)
    • Transition Orders (SGRP130; future)
  • Engage Patients & Family
    • Review patient transmitted information (VDT) (SGRP204A)
    • Patient generated data and requesting amendment (SGRP204B/D)
    • Pertinent Clinical Summaries (SGRP205)
Standards in use
  • CDA / CCDA
  • ToC CIM
  • OASIS-C
  • MDS 3.0
  • MA UTF
  • CARE Tool
  • Homecare Plan of Care 485
  • Continuity of Care CCD+/HIE
