Public Health Reporting (PHR) Initiative

Initiative Description: The Public Health Reporting (PHR) Initiative enables a standardized approach to electronic public health reporting from EHR systems to local, state and federal public health programs.

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Population Health
    • Immunization Registries (SGRP 401A)
    • Send standardized report to external mandate agency (SGRP404)
    • Report HAI (SGRP407)
Standards in use
  • HL7 V2.x
  • HL7 CDA, Data Standards (e.g. SNOMED CT, LOINC, X12)
  • Electronic Laboratory Reporting (ELR) Implementation Guide
  • FHIM
