Transitions of Care (ToC)

Initiative Description: The Transitions of Care (ToC) Initiative defines standardized content that enables electronic exchange of core clinical information among providers, patients, and other authorized entities to improve coordination of patient care. The ToC Initiative published the Companion Guide to MU2 for C-CDA after achieving consensus with the community and validating XML on various platforms. The Companion Guide provides specific, clinically and technically accurate examples of C-CDA being used properly during care transitions, aligned with MU Stage 2 criteria.

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Care Coordination
    • Summary of Care (SGRP303)
    • Referral Reports (SGRP305)
    • Interdisciplinary Problem List (SGRP127; future state)
    • Care Plan (SGRP304; future)
  • Improve Quality
    • New demographics (SGRP104)
    • Advanced Directive (SGRP112)
    • Family History (SGRP119)
    • Transition Orders (SGRP130; future)
  • Engage Patients & Family
    • Review patient transmitted information (VDT) (SGRP204A)
    • Patient generated data and requesting amendment (SGRP204B/D)
    • Pertinent Clinical Summaries (SGRP205)
Standards in use
  • C-CDA
  • CCD
  • CCR
  • HITSP C32 v2.5
  • HITSP C48
  • IHE XDS-MS
