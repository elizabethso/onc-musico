Prescription Drug Monitoring Program (PDMP)

Initiative Description: The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention estimates that over 16,500 people die each year from overdoses involving prescription painkillers. To combat this problem, many states have established Prescription Drug Monitoring Programs (PDMPs), which gather and store data on the dispensation of controlled substances within their state. PDMP systems have evolved outside the health information technology (IT) ecosystem. As a result, one of the current technical barriers is the lack of a standard interface between PDMP and health IT systems that can be used to exchange PDMP data in a timely and convenient manner.

Meaningful Use Alignment

None
Standards in use
  • ASAP
  • NIEM-PMP
  • CCDA
  • QRDA
  • NCPDP
