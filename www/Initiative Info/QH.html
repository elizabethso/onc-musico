Query Health (QH)

Initiative Description: The Query Health (QH) Initiative focuses on establishing standards for distributed queries, which can increase the ability to understand macro health trends, proactively respond to disease outbreaks, understand the efficacy of drug treatments, and contribute to reduction of healthcare costs

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Care Coordination
    • Export data/summaries (IEWG103)
  • Improve Quality
    • Query for Records (IEWG101)
  • Population Health
    • Case Reports (SGRP402B, future)
    • Send standardized adverse event report to FDA/CDC (SGRP408, future)
Standards in use
  • Health Quality Measures Format, Release 2 (HQMF R2)
  • Quality Reporting Document Architecture, Category III (QRDA III)
