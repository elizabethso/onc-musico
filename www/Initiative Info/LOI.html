Laboratory Orders Interface (LOI)

Initiative Description: The Laboratory Orders Interface (LOI) Initiative aims to standardize the content for orders sent from ambulatory EHRs to clinical laboratories

Meaningful Use Alignment

  • Improve Quality
    • Structured lab results (from MU2; potentially retire but leave as cert criteria)
    • Computerized Order Entry
Standards in use
  • HL7 V2.5.1
  • HL7 LRI IG
  • HL7 eDOS IG
  • LOINC
  • SNOMED-CT
  • ELINCS Orders
  • OID
